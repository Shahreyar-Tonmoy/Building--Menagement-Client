<h1 align="left">Description:</h1>
BuildSync Hub is a online building management system. User can agreement for rents. If owner or admin accepted users agreements then user convert in member. Member can pay with stripe payment getaway




<h1 align="left">Features:</h1>

1. Real-time Monitoring and Alerts:


 • Provide real-time monitoring of building systems, such as HVAC, lighting, security, and energy consumption.

 • Implement alerts and notifications for any anomalies or issues detected, allowing quick response to potential problems.

2. Energy Management Dashboard:


 • Include a comprehensive energy management dashboard that displays real-time and historical energy consumption data.

 • Enable users to set energy-saving goals, track progress, and receive suggestions for optimizing energy usage.


3. Asset Management:


 • Incorporate a centralized asset management system to keep track of all equipment and devices within the building.

 • Provide details on maintenance schedules, equipment lifecycle, and facilitate easy tracking of repairs and replacements.


4. User-friendly Interface with Mobile Accessibility:


 • Design an intuitive and user-friendly interface for easy navigation and control.

 • Ensure accessibility from mobile devices, allowing users to manage and monitor the building on the go.


5. Predictive Analytics and Reporting: 

 • Implement predictive analytics to forecast potential issues before they occur, helping in proactive maintenance.

 • Generate detailed reports on building performance, energy consumption, and system health to aid in decision-making and compliance reporting.




6. Responsive Design:


• A mobile-friendly design to ensure accessibility on various devices.
